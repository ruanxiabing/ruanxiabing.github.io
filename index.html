Hello!

I am a doctoral student at the Algebra, Representation, Topology (ART) team of Advanced Research Institute of Mathematics (IRMA), University of Strasbourg in Strasbourg, France. My supervisor is Vladimir Dotsenko. My research interests consist of homological algebra, algebraic operads and related subjects.

Academic Path

I did my master in Orsay Institute of Mathematics (IMO), University of Paris-Saclay in Orsay, France, from 2021 to 2023. Before, I did my bachelor at Southern University of Science and Techonology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China, from 2017 to 2021. My supervisor was Yifei Zhu.
